Utilities

Incidence and Prevalence

The utility consists of a simple representation of the incidence and prevalence of a selected chronic or acute disease group as a graphical trend over time, involving a regionally defined denominator population.
 

Health Care Activity

The utility consists of a simple representation of encounters that have occurred for a given population of patients over a selected period of time.
 

Cohort Results Trend Dashboard

This utility compares cohort results (i.e. quality indicators) between organisations trended over a period of time. The results are displayed as a trended percentage of the enumerator cohort compared to the denominator cohort.

Frailty Checker

This utility checks the frailty status for a given patient. It is used to test the NHS111 Frailty API. If the specified patient has an observation for severe or moderate frailty then the utility API returns a value of TRUE.
