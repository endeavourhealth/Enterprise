Utilities

Incidence and Prevalence

The utility consists of a simple representation of the incidence and prevalence of a selected chronic or acute disease group as a graphical trend over time, involving a regionally defined denominator population.
 

Health Care Activity

The utility consists of a simple representation of encounters that have occurred for a given population of patients over a selected period of time.
 

Quality Indicator Dashboard

This utility compares quality indicators between organisations trended over a period of time.
